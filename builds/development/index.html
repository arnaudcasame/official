A brief bio of Arnaud Casamé

Computer technologies have always fascinated me, specially web development ones. This brought my curiosity to the highest level possible and motivated me to become the best self-taught web developer. I spend my whole day, practically every day, experimenting with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript; dabbling with PHP, MySQL, MongoDB, Firebase and Python; and inhaling a wide variety of articles on Medium, Quora and StackOverFlow. I build from static websites to data-driven web apps with high level of complexity and I DO IT WELL.

As I said, I'm curious, and I enjoy work that challenges me to learn something new and stretch in a different direction. I do my best to stay on top of changes in the state of the art so that I can meet challenges with tools well suited to the job at hand. My personal projects on my GitHub will give you a better idea of the techs that I master and the tools that I use.
